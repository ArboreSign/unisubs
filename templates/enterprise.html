  • Quality Control

    Amara’s Flexible Workflows offer several ways to ensure outstanding quality for your subtitles. Whether you use internal staff, outside contractors, professional services, or a volunteer community -- you’ll be able to create, review, and approve workflows that meet your needs.

    Examples of QC processes:

    • Have your contractors work inside your Amara account and then require staff review before subtitles go live.
    • Some universities use paid captioning services to quickly caption lecture videos and then invite experts in the university community to fix any mistakes with proper names or technical terms.
    • Volunteer teams can self-manage and reach very high quality by requiring peer review of subtitles before they are published. More senior members of the community can be promoted to a moderator role.

  • Automated Workflows

    Workflows enable automating the flow of content through different authoring groups, including: volunteers, private crowds (staff, employees, students, etc), and professionals. They can be used for both first pass (authoring), as well as subsequent review passes for Quality Control.

    Different workflows can be chained together to create an unlimited number of highly flexible configurations. The Amara team can help you determine the best workflow for your subtitle processes and integrate it with your existing publishing platform.

    Contractors with Staff Review

    Use captioning and transcription contractors to create your subtitles, then require staff review and approval before they are published back to your server. Remember, no one sees your subtitles until YOU publish it.

    Crowdsourced subtitles, followed by staff review

    In many cases, organizations want to leverage the power of the crowd, but don’t want to leave any chance for poor quality captions or subtitles to appear on their content. This workflow keeps videos private until a staff member has approved the subtitles.

    Professionally created English captions, crowd translation

    Amara offers professional- quality captions at very low cost with quick turn-around times. English can often be done in less than a day. Once English is completed, it’s a snap to request translations from volunteers or staff. We have seen videos translated into multiple languages in just days -- through a volunteer crowd.

    Crowd translations, with professional quality-control

    Amara is, quite simply, the most cost-effective way to get professional-level quality for foreign language video subtitles. By leveraging professional quality review, you can expect to pay roughly 75% less than the market price for traditional professional subtitles.
