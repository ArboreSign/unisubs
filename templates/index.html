What can you do with Amara?

Create subtitles yourself!

You can add any youtube, vimeo, mp4, flv, or html5 audio of video to amara, and subtitle it yourself, or ask a friend to help.

Join a volunteer community

Exceptional organizations like GirlRising and WorldVision need your help translating and captioning.

Purchase captions and subtitles

We create professional captions and subtitles at affordable prices. Film distributors like ro*co have purchased captions for speedy delivery to netflix.

Make your video global

Video is a powerful way to spread messages — we can help you translate it! Epic Rap Battles of History videos have been translated into more than 60 languages and shared around the world in just days!

Engage your crowd

Amara help you harness the power of a crowd. Teams like Udacity have more than 1500 members captioning their online eductional videos.

Easy platform integration

We have a powerful API and support most video platforms and subtitle formats. TED.com gets their subtitles from Amara and keeps their websites and mobile apps up to date.
